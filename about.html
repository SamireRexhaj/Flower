Saola Through Time: Unveiling the Floral Legacy of Our Beloved Flower Shop


"In the heartwarming tale of our flower shop's origins, two kindred spirits, lifelong friends since high school, embarked on a blossoming journey that would forever change their lives. United by a shared passion for flowers, their bond deepened with every carefully chosen bouquet exchanged during birthdays, milestones, and even mundane days. As fate would have it, on the cusp of turning 20, the two friends, fueled by their mutual love for blooms, took a leap of faith and decided to transform their shared dream into reality. With enthusiasm and a youthful zeal, they opened the doors to our beloved flower shop, a testament to the enduring power of friendship and the timeless beauty found in each petal that graces our humble space."
